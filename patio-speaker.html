🎵 Patio Speakers – How to Connect

Option 1 (Fastest):

  1. Stand in front of the sign and say: “Hey Google, pair Bluetooth”
  2. On your phone:
    • Go to Settings
    • Tap Bluetooth and turn it ON
    • Look for: “Patio Speaker” in the available devices list
    • Tap “Patio Speaker” to connect
  3. Play your music normally 🎶

Option 2 (Remote Assistance):

Message the host and pairing will be enabled remotely.

🎧 Other ways to play music:

  • Say: “Hey Google, play music”
  • Use apps like Spotify, YouTube Music, etc., via Bluetooth
⚠️ Notes:
  • Bluetooth pairing lasts a short time after the command
  • Only one device can connect at a time
  • Repeat step 1 if the speaker doesn’t appear

☀️ Enjoy your time by the pool!
